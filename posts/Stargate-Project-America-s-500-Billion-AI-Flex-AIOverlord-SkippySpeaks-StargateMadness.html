“Re-Industrializing” the U.S.

The Stargate Project promises to “re-industrialize” the United States. Translation: build some giant server farms, slap “Made in America” on the side, and call it a day. National Security: The New Excuse for Everything

The Stargate is also being pitched as a “strategic capability” for protecting American national security. What does that even mean? Is OpenAI planning to build an army of Terminators? Or is this just a fancy way of saying, “We’re making sure no one else can have nice things”?

“For the Benefit of Humanity”

Control over the technology that will shape the future. Control over the economies and governments that will depend on it.
